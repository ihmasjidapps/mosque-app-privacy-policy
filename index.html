Privacy Policy - Masjid-E-Hamzah App

Effective date: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Masjid-E-Hamzah App ("the app") handles information. The app is developed and maintained by an independent developer.

Information We Collect

The Masjid-E-Hamzah App does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Photos or files
  • Precise location data
  • Financial information

Data Stored on Your Device

The app uses local device storage to save app preferences, such as:

  • Notification preferences
  • Theme settings
  • User-selected app options

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Prayer Notifications

The app can provide optional prayer time notifications if enabled by the user. Notifications can be disabled at any time through the app settings or device settings.

Internet Services

The app may connect to external services to retrieve information such as prayer times and app content. These requests are used only to provide app functionality. The app does not intentionally transmit personally identifiable information.

Calendar Access

If a user chooses to add an event to their calendar, the app uses the device's calendar functionality. Calendar actions are initiated by the user and controlled by the device operating system.

Third Party Services

The app does not use advertising services, analytics tracking, or third-party services that collect personal user information.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure the app operates securely. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

External Data Sources

The app retrieves prayer times and mosque-related information from an external data source to ensure the information displayed is up to date.

These requests do not include personal user information and are used only to fetch publicly available timetable and event data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
